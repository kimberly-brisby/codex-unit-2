Notes on the Box Model

  • Layer One

    • Content

    The content area is were text and images go.
  • Layer Two

    • Padding

    The padding area is the space inside and before the border.
  • Layer Three

    • Border

    The border is the space around the padding.
  • Layer Four

    • Margin

    Lastly the margin sits outside the border.

When done correctly a layered box will form around the content.
